Data Bomb

In this project, my group worked with the National Museum of Scotland and our "bomb", which was designed and built for data donation, was invited to display in the Science Festival of Edinburgh.
















Corpus

Corpus was a project based upon Quorate Technology data, which generates keyword summaries of speech recordings made at the Conference on World Affairs. We used digital technology to visualize the data and to showcase our thoughts about semantic search.








Noise Island

This project was supervised by Jonathan Mills, former director of the Edinburgh International Festival. The installation was held successfully in the main building of Edinburgh College of Art.
